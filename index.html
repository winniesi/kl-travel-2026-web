Petaling Street (Chinatown)

Walk through history in the heart of KL, where traditional lanterns hang over modern cafes and historic temples.

Why This Place? / 推荐理由:
A sensory overload of street food, smells, and history. It's where the city's past meets its vibrant present. / 历史与充满活力的现状在这里交汇，是感受市井气息的最佳去处。
Local Flavor

Authentic Nasi Lemak

The unofficial national dish of Malaysia—fragrant coconut rice served with spicy sambal, crispy anchovies, and peanuts.

Why This Place? / 推荐理由:
No trip is complete without it. It's the flavor of Malaysia in a single plate. / 马来西亚的国民美食，只有吃过椰浆饭，才算完整地体验了这里。
